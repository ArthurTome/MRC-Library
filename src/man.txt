mrc program compute mobile radio channels and output
channel, correlation or fft of channel.

INPUTS
    --help  help of mrc program

    --ncis  number of cisoids terms

    --freq  max frequency of channel

    --sig   standat deviation of channel

    --size  size of vector

    --ts    samples time step

OUTPUTS
    --soc   channels real and complex vectors

    --rxx   real correlation vector

    --fft   fourrier transform of auto correlation
